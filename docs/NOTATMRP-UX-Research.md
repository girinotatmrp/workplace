# NOT@MRP Overview

NOT@MRP is a hyperlocal digital platform designed to empower local merchants, shoppers, and communities by integrating neighborhood commerce, quick delivery, and local event experiences through a unified ecosystem.

---

## **1.Laundry (Local Service Commerce)**

**Definition:**

A digital marketplace enabling local laundry service providers to list their offerings online, facilitating neighborhood booking and direct merchant-customer engagement.

**Business Model:**

- Revenue from commissions on each laundry order processed.
- Subscription fees from laundry merchants for premium storefronts, enhanced visibility, and promotional tools.
- Advertising fees for featured listings and targeted promotions.
- Focus on digitizing and growing neighborhood laundry services, supporting local livelihoods.

---

## 2. LocalMart (Quick Commerce & Delivery)

**Definition:**

A rapid delivery service connecting local grocery stores and essential retailers with nearby customers seeking fast, reliable delivery (typically within 30 minutes).

**Business Model:**

- Delivery fees charged to users.
- Commissions on sales processed through the platform.
- Subscription plans or priority listing for participating retailers.
- Leveraging local store inventory to reduce delivery times and costs, promoting convenience.

---

## 3. Events (Local Experiences & Ticketing)

**Definition:**

A community-driven event platform for listing, promoting, and ticketing local events, cultural festivals, workshops, and social gatherings in neighborhoods.

**Business Model:**

- Commissions on ticket sales and booking fees.
- Paid promotional campaigns and premium event listings for organizers.
- Value-added services such as VIP passes or merchandise sales integration.
- Strengthening community engagement by fostering local experiences.

---

## Summary

NOT@MRP integrates these three verticals to form a comprehensive hyperlocal ecosystem that digitizes neighborhood commerce, expedites access to essentials, and builds vibrant communities through local events — all aimed at sustaining and growing local economies while delivering convenience and rich user experiences.
